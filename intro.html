What is ESCIP about?

  • ESCIP is a collaborative initiative dedicated to providing easy access to Jupyter Notebooks for chemistry, physics and astronomy education. All content is open source but we still do highly apprecite any credit to ESCIP.

  • We provide trainings for instructors new to python and hold regular workshops at universities across the nation.
